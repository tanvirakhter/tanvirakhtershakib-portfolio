About

Short overview about me

Don't know me?

Well this is me in a nutshell.

Beginning my academic journey at Brunel University London in 2020, I pursued Computer Science with a specialisation in Software Engineering. After years of hard work and dedication, I am set to graduate in summer 2023.
BrunelPlus Gold Award
With the slogan "Beyond Brunel: be more than your degree" The Brunel+ Award is designed to give recognition for the non-academic activities that one participates during their time at Brunel. Students can receive a Bronze+, Silver+ or Gold+ Award to demonstrate to employers that you are a well-rounded candidate with skills beyond academia. I was recognised for my achievements, winning the prestigious BrunelPlus Gold Award.
Environment & Ethics officer
Union of Brunel Students elect a team of officers and representatives each year; to represent Brunel students, champion issues that are important to students, and to have a positive impact on campus. I was elected the Environment & Ethics officer for the academic year 2022-23. As a part-time Union Staff I had opportunity to represent Brunel Students and polish my leadership skill.
Brunel Volunteers Silver Award
I was active in different Clubs and Societies at Brunel University London. I was the Vice-chair of Brunel Bangladesh Society where I organised 30+ events in the academic year of 2022-23. I was also active in sports club. I was the Secretary for the Brunel Rowing club where I organised regular practice sessions.
Industrial Placement
After completing my second year, I had landed an industrial placement at a prominent security firm in London named RMSS LTD. I worked as an Information Technology Intern in there. I had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects in accordance with my role and develop initiatives for the growth and development of the company. This tremendous opportunity granted me deeper insights into the IT business while interacting with key stakeholders within the company. In addition, working on these various projects was a huge reward as I was able to gain some insightful experience that I might apply to any career I undertake.
